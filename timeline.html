The Road to May 31

Every step from today to the night the room blooms
Friday, May 22

We begin

The plan is set and we are partners. Your event page is already live and ready to share.

This weekend, May 23 to 24

Your part

Fill in the requirements sheet, and send your colors, your book covers, and your logins. Then rest a little.

Monday, May 25

The invitations go out

Your invitation email reaches your people. The seat-fill posts begin, warm and steady.

Tuesday to Wednesday, May 26 to 27

The warm push

One or two gentle posts a day. Flyers go up at the theater and around town.

Wednesday, May 27

A note to your guests

The Know Before You Go email is queued for everyone holding a ticket, so they arrive ready.

Thursday to Friday, May 28 to 29

A quiet window

Everything is pre-scheduled. Nothing is asked of you. You rest before the big night.

Saturday, May 30

Last call

One final, gentle reminder to anyone still deciding. The room is nearly full.

Sunday, May 31

Petals & Poetry

Half past seven in the evening. The doors open, the welcome drinks are poured, and the room blooms. This is the night.

Monday, June 1

A thank-you

A warm note to everyone who came. We breathe, and we look at what we learned.

June and beyond

Phase two

Your new website comes to life, and we plan the soft-launch party for your new book.

Chin up, as you head on your way.
